# Privacy Notice (Public Beta)

WexDE ("we", "our", or "us") operates the WexSay application. This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data during the WexSay Public Beta program.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

To run our real-time messaging service, we collect and process the following data:
* **Account Credentials:** Your phone number, email address, username, and profile display name.
* **Messaging & Media Data:** Your text messages and media files are stored securely on our servers. This is required to deliver messages to offline users and sync chat histories across your devices.
* **Contacts Sync:** We sync telephone numbers from your device's address book to identify which of your contacts are using WexSay. We do not store your contacts' raw names or profile details on our servers.
* **WebRTC Metadata:** Temporary session metadata required to establish voice and video calls. Calling streams are routed directly peer-to-peer (P2P) when possible and are not recorded on our servers.
* **Device telemetry:** Standard logging details (app version, OS version, device model, and crash logs) to identify and fix beta program bugs.

---

## 2. How We Protect Your Data

* **Encryption in Transit:** All communications between the WexSay client app and our backend APIs are encrypted using TLS 1.3.
* **Encryption at Rest:** Sensitive device tokens, access keys, and local caches are stored on your device using AES-256 GCM encryption.
* **Server-Side Security:** Backend databases are hosted on private, firewall-protected virtual private servers (VPS) with restricted administrative access.

---

## 3. Data Retention & Deletion

* Chat messages and files are stored on our servers for as long as your account remains active.
* You can request account deletion at any time by contacting us at `privacy@wexsay.com` or via our in-app support channel.
* Upon account deletion, all your personal profile details, active chats, and media records will be permanently removed from our active databases.

---

## 4. Updates to This Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice to reflect changes in our app's features or security practices. We will notify beta users of major updates via our in-app official announcement channel.
