# Getting Support

Thank you for testing WexSay! If you run into issues, need assistance, or want to ask questions, this document guides you on how to get support.

---

## 💬 Community Support (Recommended)

For general questions, configuration help, and discussion, please use our community channels:

* **GitHub Discussions:** Visit [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/Wex-DE/WexSay/discussions) to ask questions, share tips, and connect with other beta testers.
* **Bug Tracking:** Check if your issue is already listed in [Known Issues](KNOWN_ISSUES.md) or active [Issues](https://github.com/Wex-DE/WexSay/issues). If not, open a new bug report.

---

## 🛡️ Official verified Chat (In-App Support)

If you are logged into the WexSay mobile application, you can receive support directly within the app:

1. Locate the pinned **WexSay Official Channel** at the top of your chat list.
2. If you need assistance, use our automated FAQ query panel inside the official chat.
3. If your query requires human moderation, a member of our support team will open a direct chat session in that window.

---

## 📧 Private Support Email

For account recovery problems, email registration issues, or general administrative queries, you can contact our support team privately:

* **Email:** `support@wexsay.com`
* **Response Time:** We aim to respond within 24-48 hours.

---

## 🔒 Security Exploits

If you discover a security vulnerability or exploit:
* **Do NOT post it publicly.**
* Refer to [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for reporting guidelines.
